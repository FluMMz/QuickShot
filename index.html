How It Works

From installation to finished screenshot in under 30 seconds

1

Install Extension

One-time installation from Chrome Web Store. No configuration needed - QuickShot works immediately.

2

Press Hotkey

Ctrl+Shift+Q starts screenshot mode. Alternatively use the extension icon in your browser.

3

Select Area

Drag your mouse over the desired area. Live preview shows you exactly what will be captured.

4

Optional: Annotate

Click "Edit" for annotation tools or "Upload" for direct upload. Arrows, text and highlights in seconds.
